Questions you probably have

Is it really free?
Yes. The full audit — interview, analysis, flow diagrams, and PDF report — costs nothing. No credit card, no trial period, no upsell waiting at the end.
Do I need to know my process perfectly before I start?
No. The AI interviewer is designed to draw out the real process — including the informal steps and workarounds that never make it into a written procedure. Just describe what actually happens, not what's supposed to happen.
Is my business information private?
Your session data is used to generate your report. We anonymise collected data and may share anonymised data with third parties for purposes such as service improvement and research. We do not share personally identifiable information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How is this different from hiring a consultant?
A process consultant costs $150–$400/hour and takes weeks. This takes 10 minutes and is free. The honest tradeoff: a consultant can shadow your team, run group interviews, and observe the work directly. FlowNext works best for getting a fast, clear picture of one specific process — and figuring out whether a deeper engagement is worth it.
Can I audit more than one process?
Yes. Each audit is a fresh session. Run as many as you like — one for invoicing, one for onboarding, one for hiring. Each produces its own separate report.
