Common questions

Is it really free?
Yes. The full audit — interview, analysis, flow diagrams, and PDF report — is free. No credit card, no trial period, no catch.
Do I need to know my process perfectly before I start?
No. The AI interviewer is designed to draw out the real process — including the informal steps and workarounds that never make it into a written procedure. Just describe what actually happens, not what's supposed to happen.
Is my business information private?
Your session data is used only to generate your report. We don't sell or share your business information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How is this different from hiring a consultant?
A process consultant typically costs $150–$400/hour and takes weeks to deliver findings. This audit takes 10 minutes and is free. The tradeoff: a consultant can interview your whole team and observe the work firsthand. This is best for getting a fast, clear picture of one specific process — and deciding whether a deeper engagement is even necessary.
Can I audit more than one process?
Yes. Each audit is a fresh session. Run as many as you like — one for invoicing, one for onboarding, one for hiring. Each produces its own separate report.
